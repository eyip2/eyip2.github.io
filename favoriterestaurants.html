I really enjoy trying new restaurants! Since moving here in August, I have tried 84 new establishments in NYC. Here are my top 10 favorites thus far according to Beli:

Restaurant Beli Rating Notes
1 Take 31 10 Fun, unique, affordable Korean dishes
2 Forma Pasta Factory 10 High quality pasta dishes all for $14
3 Raku 9.7 Cozy and affordable handmade udon restaurant, noodle texture is incredible
4 Her Name is Han 9.7 Sister restaurant to Take 31, cozy restaurant, fun and modern take on Korean cuisine
5 Bangkok Supper Club 9.7 Extremely well done take on modern Thai food, although quite pricey
6 Shinzo Omakase 9.7 $69 13-course omakase, BYOB, staff treats each other like family
7 Prince Street Pizza 9.5 The Spicy Spring is the best pizza I've ever had in my life. Not Lucali's, not anything in Italy.
8 Szechuan Mountain House 9.4 Cozy and well done Szechuanese food at reasonable prices
9 Taqueria Ramirez 9.1 Mexico City style tacos, fun, clean, and cozy casual joint
10 konban NYC 9 Known for their katsu and other small shareables, cozy and contemporary Japanese restaurant
